Why Mega Polybag is Gujarat's #1 Choice

Agriculture & Fertilizer Bags

Farmers across Saurashtra and Gujarat trust our bags, often called "Mazboot Bori" or "Desi Bachka". We are a leading supplier of Fertilizer Bags in Morbi and Seed Bags that protect crops from moisture. Our strong stitching ensures that heavy loads (50kg+) are safe during transport.

Food Grade & Aata Bags

Hygiene is our priority. We manufacture the finest Aata Bag in Gujarat using food-grade virgin polymer. Our high-clarity BOPP bags are perfect as a premium Rice Bag, keeping grains fresh and insect-free. Perfect for brands looking for "Product Safety" and shelf appeal.

Industrial & Chemical Packaging

Morbi is the hub of ceramics, and we supply the strongest Tile Adhesive Bag in Morbi. For hazardous materials, our Chemical Bag in Morbi comes with a safety liner to prevent leakage. We ensure your expensive industrial powders get maximum "Product Protection" during rough handling.

Proudly Make in India

Mega Polybag supports the Make in India initiative. All our manufacturing happens locally in Patidar Estate, Morbi. By choosing us, you support local manufacturing while getting world-class "Export Quality" packaging that ensures 100% safety for your goods.
