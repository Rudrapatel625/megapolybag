More Than Just a Bag: The Journey of Your Product

When a manufacturer in Morbi ships Tile Adhesive or Chemicals, that product often travels hundreds of kilometers. It might go by truck to other states, or to ports like Mundra and Kandla for export. In that journey, your packaging is the only armor your product has.

At Mega Polybag, we don't just "make bags." We engineer protection.

Why "Cheap" Packaging is Expensive

Many businesses try to save a few paise by using low-GSM woven sacks. But consider the cost of failure:

  • Moisture Damage: Cement and chemicals turn into rock if humidity seeps in.
  • Bursting: A bag that bursts during loading at the port means lost product and a damaged reputation.
  • Fading: If your brand logo fades in the sun, customers lose trust in the quality inside.

The Mega Polybag Advantage (BOPP & PP)

We combine the strength of Polypropylene (PP) with the high-definition finish of Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP). This creates a "sandwich" of strength:

  1. Inner Layer: Woven fabric that resists tearing under heavy weight (25kg - 50kg).
  2. Outer Layer: A laminated film that locks out moisture and makes your colors shine.
