Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use for PP & BOPP bags?

We use 100% virgin polypropylene granules. For BOPP bags, high-quality laminated films are used for superior print and durability.

Can you customize size, printing and GSM?

Yes. Bag size, fabric GSM, liner, valve option and printing can be customized exactly as per your product requirement.

What industries do you serve?

Cement, fertilizer, rice, food grains, seeds, cattle feed, construction materials and retail packaging.

Do you supply all over India?

Yes. We supply PAN India with strong logistics support from our Morbi (Gujarat) manufacturing unit.
