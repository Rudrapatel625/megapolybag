What are PP Cement Bags?

PP cement bags are woven polypropylene bags used for safe storage and transportation of cement and construction materials.

Material Used

  • 100% Virgin Polypropylene
  • Optional LDPE liner
  • UV stabilized fabric

Why PP Cement Bags are Important

  • High load bearing capacity
  • Moisture protection
  • Cost effective and recyclable

Specifications

  • Capacity: 25kg / 50kg
  • GSM: 70 – 120
  • Printing: Up to 6 colors

Applications

  • OPC Cement
  • PPC Cement
  • Construction Material
